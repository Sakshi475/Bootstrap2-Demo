Noddy

Noddy is an English character created by English children's author Enid Blyton. He is a wooden toy doll depicted as having a childlike view of the world..

Oswald

Oswald is a safety-conscious but fun-loving octopus who lives in an apartment complex in Big City with his dog, Weenie.

Mr.Bean

The childish Mr Bean uses his unusual wit to fulfill everyday tasks. But, more often than not, he creates trouble for himself and those around him.
